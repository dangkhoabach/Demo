About Lanoca Design

Lanoca Limited Company was established in 2021 and is headquartered in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. Based on our knowledge and experiences design industry, we aim to provide our clients with creative ideas and outstanding, high standard of beauty, above and beyond expectations. We also offer the highest quality construction services at reasonable and competitive prices.
